Engineering Material Cost Analysis

Effective UI design in engineering requires an understanding of the underlying data. Our system uses specific mathematical notations to calculate material longevity and replacement costs.

Cost Calculation Formula:

The total estimated cost (C) is derived using the summation of individual material units (q) multiplied by unit price (p), plus overhead (O):

C = Σ (q_i × p_i) + O
